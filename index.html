Financial Management for Freelancers

Your all-in-one iOS app for tracking income/expenses, budgets, invoices, mileage, taxes, and more—with seamless business/personal separation.

Learn More View on GitHub

Everything You Need to Manage Your Finances

Dashboard & Overview

Real-time summaries of income, expenses, budgets, invoices, mileage, and tax estimates with business/personal filtering.

Transactions Management

Add/edit/list transactions with auto-categorization, receipt matching, and recurring automation for business/personal finances.

Receipts & Scanning

OCR scanning, intelligent splitting, matching to transactions, and tax deduction suggestions—all stored securely on-device.

Budgets Management

Create/track budgets by type (envelope, zero-based) with rollovers, warnings, and integration with recurring transactions.

Invoices & Clients

Generate invoices, manage clients, send reminders, track payments, and generate aging reports with stats.

Mileage & Taxes

Auto/manual tracking with IRS deductions, tax estimates using 2025 brackets, and year-end summaries.

Powered by Apple Technologies

Modern iOS Development Stack

FLO is built using Apple's latest frameworks for secure, efficient financial management on iOS.

  • SwiftUI for intuitive user interfaces
  • SwiftData for secure local storage
  • VisionKit for receipt OCR scanning
  • CoreLocation for mileage tracking
  • UserNotifications for timely reminders
